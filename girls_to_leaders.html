Welcome

Girls to Leaders is a nonprofit and community-based organization founded by Anesu Mhembere, committed to the leadership growth, development, and empowerment of young girls in Africa. We envision a future where girls rise into strong, compassionate, and confident leaders.

About Us

Founded with love and purpose, Girls to Leaders was born out of a desire to break cycles and build strong futures. Our programs focus on mentorship, education, and community engagement, providing girls with the tools and encouragement they need to lead.

Our Mission

To nurture and inspire the leadership potential of young girls in Africa by providing holistic support in education, mentorship, spiritual growth, and skill development, rooted in values of faith, strength, and dignity.

Initiatives

  • Mentorship Circles: Pairing young girls with women leaders in their communities.
  • Leadership Bootcamps: Training sessions and workshops on confidence, public speaking, and advocacy.
  • Faith & Future: Spiritual empowerment through scripture and fellowship.
  • Skill Saturdays: Hands-on learning in entrepreneurship, digital literacy, and creative arts.
